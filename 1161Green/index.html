Full Description

This classic Edwardian condominium blends timeless San Francisco character with thoughtful modern updates. Located in the heart of Russian Hill, the home sits on a picturesque tree-lined street just half a block from the cable car line and moments from neighborhood cafés, boutiques, and parks.

The formal living room showcases a decorative fireplace, tall coved ceilings, and a beautiful stained-glass window. An elegant dining room with built-in china cabinet and bay-window seat provides an inviting space for gatherings. The designer kitchen features Calacatta-style stone countertops, premium appliances, and ample storage.

Two tranquil rear bedrooms offer a quiet retreat, each with generous closet space. The renovated bathroom includes a soaking tub, basketweave tile flooring, and marble-topped vanity. Additional conveniences include in-unit laundry and private basement storage.

Residents enjoy access to a shared rooftop deck with sweeping views of the Golden Gate Bridge, the Bay, and Sutro Tower — the perfect spot for morning coffee or sunset unwinding.
